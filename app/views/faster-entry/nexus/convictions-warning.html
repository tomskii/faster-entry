You might be able to apply for Nexus

You might still be able to apply for Nexus if you have a minor conviction that is more than 10 years old.

However, the Canadian and US authorities will consider all criminal offences, including spent ones, when making a Nexus application decision. This means if you have an older or spent conviction you still may not pass the UK background checks and won’t get a refund of the £42 fee.

Contact the Canada Border Services Agency or US Customs and Border ProtectionOpens in new window for question about how past convictions may affect your application.
